# UniverStar

The word _UniverStar_ is a combination of **University** and **Star**.

## Introduction

This project aims to provide a platform for university students to showcase and promote their projects. The goal is to enable students to gain more recognition and stars for their work, enhancing their competitiveness for future careers.

## Features

- **Student-Oriented Display:** Student can submit their projects under their respective institutions, ensuring the projects are categorized by school.
- **Discoverability:** Students can explore projects from different courses, gaining insight into course content or get inspiration for their own assignments or projects.
- **Career Advantage:** By showing their work, students increase their visibility to potential employers and recruiters.

## Potential Users

- **Students:** Showcase projects, gain recognition, and build a portfolio for job application or further study.
- **Employers & Recruiters:** Discover talented students and evaluate their skills through project work.
- **University:** Highlight student achievements and provide references for courses or programs.

## Submit Your Project

If your institution has been added, please kindly refer to the [`docs/submit-project.md`](docs/submit-project.md).

Otherwise, you may [open an issue](https://github.com/universtar-org/universtar/issues/new?template=instituion-request.md) to provide information of your school so that maintainers could add your institution.
